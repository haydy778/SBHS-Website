Chess Club Welcome

Dear Chess Enthusiasts,
We are excited to welcome you to the School Chess Club! Whether you're a seasoned player or a beginner, our club is the perfect place to enhance your skills, challenge your mind, and meet fellow chess lovers.
Join us every Tuesday at lunchtime in 3p for engaging activities, including tournaments, friendly matches, and strategy sessions. Our experienced players are here to guide you, and we have plenty of resources to help you improve your game.

In addition to regular meetings, we will host workshops, puzzles, and seminars with guest speakers to broaden your chess knowledge. This is your chance to learn new tactics, practice regularly, and enjoy the timeless game of chess in a supportive environment.
We look forward to seeing you at our next meeting and sharing many exciting matches and learning experiences together.
