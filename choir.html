Open Choir

Momentum

Momentum is a vibrant school choir celebrated for its enthusiastic performances at major events such as The Big Sing in Invercargill and the annual school prizegiving. Founded with a vision to create an inclusive musical environment, Momentum has become a cornerstone of the school's cultural life, offering students a platform to express themselves, develop musical talents, and form lasting friendships.

A highlight of Momentum's calendar is The Big Sing, a national choral competition in Invercargill. This event attracts school choirs from across the country, providing a stage for Momentum to showcase their hard work and creativity. Preparation for The Big Sing begins months in advance with rigorous rehearsals under the guidance of choir director Mr. Michael Buick. Known for his inspiring leadership, Mr Buick selects a diverse range of pieces, ensuring the repertoire is both challenging and engaging. Momentum's performances at The Big Sing are marked by vibrant energy, impeccable harmonies, and visible joy, earning them accolades and admiration.

In addition to The Big Sing, Momentum is central to the school's annual prizegiving ceremony. This event celebrates student achievements, and the choir's performance is a highlight. Their repertoire for the prizegiving includes uplifting anthems and heartfelt ballads, reflecting the celebratory and reflective nature of the occasion.

Momentum's role extends beyond performances; it fosters a sense of community and belonging. Open to all students, the choir emphasizes inclusivity and mutual support. Social events, workshops, and retreats strengthen bonds among members, creating a supportive environment.

Competitive Choir

Dimensions

Dimensions is a highly competitive school choir renowned for its exceptional performances at major events such as The Big Sing in Invercargill, the annual school prizegiving, and the prestigious regional Cadenza Choir event. Established with the aim of achieving musical excellence, Dimensions has earned a reputation for its rigorous training and high standards, becoming a celebrated institution within the school and beyond.

A highlight of Dimensions' annual calendar is The Big Sing, a national choral competition held in Invercargill. This event attracts school choirs from across the country, providing Dimensions with an opportunity to showcase their dedication, skill, and creativity. Preparation for The Big Sing involves months of intense rehearsals under the direction of Mr. Michael Buick, the choir's esteemed conductor. Known for his meticulous attention to detail and ability to inspire, Mr. Buick selects a challenging and diverse repertoire, pushing the choir to excel. Dimensions' performances at The Big Sing are characterized by their technical precision, emotional depth, and powerful harmonies, earning them high praise and numerous accolades.

Last year, Dimensions achieved a significant milestone by participating in the regional Cadenza Choir event. This prestigious competition brought together top school choirs from the region, offering a platform for Dimensions to further demonstrate their excellence. Their performance at Cadenza was marked by intricate arrangements, flawless execution, and a captivating stage presence, solidifying their status as one of the leading school choirs in the region.

Junior Choir

Unison

Unison is a junior school choir composed of enthusiastic Year 7 and 8 students, known for its heartwarming performances at the annual school prizegiving and the Sing Out event. Formed to introduce younger students to the joys of choral singing, Unison emphasizes the importance of teamwork, musicality, and fun.

The choir's name, Unison, reflects its core value of coming together to create a harmonious and unified sound. Under the guidance of their energetic conductor, Mr. Michael Buick, the choir practices a diverse repertoire that includes both contemporary songs and classic pieces tailored to young voices. Mr. Buick's nurturing approach ensures that each student feels confident and supported.

Unison's performance at the school prizegiving is a cherished tradition, celebrating the accomplishments of the students. Their songs, chosen to reflect the spirit of celebration and achievement, resonate with the audience, adding a special touch to the ceremony.

Another highlight for Unison is their participation in Sing Out, a local event that brings together junior choirs from various schools. This event provides Unison with a platform to showcase their talents, learn from their peers, and experience the joy of community singing.
